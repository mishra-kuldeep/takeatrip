Northeast

5 NIGHT NORTH BENGAL & SIKKIM

SAMPLE ITINERARY: (DARJEELING 2N - KALIMPONG 1N - GANGTOK 2N)

NJP RLY STATION / IXB AIRPORT - DARJEELING (75 KMS. / 3 HRS.)

Day 01: Assistance on arrival at New Jalpaiguri Railway Station/ Bagdogra International Airport and transfer to Darjeeling (6,950 Ft.). On arrival check-in to the hotel and rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

DARJEELING FULL DAY SIGHTSEEING WITH TIGER HILL

Day 02: Early morning (4:00 AM) visit Tiger Hill and then back to hotel. After breakfast, starting at 8.30 AM, visit Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, P.N. Zoological Park (Thursday closed), Tenzing Rock, Ropeway, Tibetan Refugee self-help Centre (Sunday closed), Tea Garden (outer view), Ghoom Monastery and Batasia Loop. Evening free for shopping or leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

DARJEELING - KALIMPONG - LOCAL SIGHTSEEING (77 KMS. / 6½ HRS.)

Day 03:  After breakfast, transfer to Kalimpong (4,091 Ft. / 55 Kms. / 2½ Hrs.). On arrival check-in to your hotel. Afternoon enjoy half day tour of Kalimpong visiting Mangal Dham, Deolo Hill, Golf Course, Durpin Dara Hills. Overnight stay at Kalimpong.

KALIMPONG - GANGTOK - HALF DAY LOCAL SIGHTSEEING (108 KMS. / 7 HRS.)

Day 04: Post breakfast, transfer to Gangtok (5,500 Ft. / 78 Kms. / 3 Hrs.). On arrival, check-in to your hotel. After that half day Gangtok city tour visiting Flower show, Cottage Industry and Handicraft Centre (Closed on Sunday and 1st and 3rd Saturday of the month), Institute of Tibetology (Closed on Sunday), Dro-dul Chorten, (maximum 3-4 hours). Overnight stay at Gangtok.

EXCURSION TO TSOMGO LAKE AND BABA MANDIR (53 KMS. / 3 HRS. ONE-WAY)

Day 05:  After breakfast, start for an excursion to Tsomgo Lake (12,400 ft. / 37 Kms. / 02 Hrs. from Gangtok) and further drive to Baba Mandir (13,200 ft. / 16 Kms. / 01 Hrs. from Tsomgo Lake). Evening back to Gangtok. Overnight stay at Gangtok

GANGTOK - NJP RLY STATION / IXB AIRPORT (128 KMS. / 5 HRS.)

Day 06: After breakfast, checkout from the hotel and take the departure transfers to New Jalpaiguri Railway Station / Bagdogra International Airport for your onward journey.

  • Nathulla Pass remains closed on Monday and Tuesday.
  • Nathulla Pass Cannot be committed till the last hour due to complication on permit issues. Even though we will try our level best to fulfil the guest’s requirement)
  • In case of Land slide or any reason Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir / North Sikkim (Lachen & amp; Lachung) is closed we will provide alternate sightseeing.
  • Luxury Xylo / Innova / Scorpio/Similar (6 Seater)
  • Regular Tata Sumo / Maxx/Similar (8 Seater only as per Sikkim traffic rules)
  • Small Wagon-R / Alto / Omni Van/Similar (4 Seater)

The itinerary can be tailor made according to your need and budget. Let us know the details i.e no of days, no of pax, places to be covered etc. and we will come up with the program accordingly.
