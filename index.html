About Us

Welcome to the world of Take A Trip....

Based in Kolkata and Port Blair, Take A Trip is a leading Andaman Destination Management Company delivering bespoke experience for corporate clients. Since inception, we provide B2B catering services to our corporate clients keeping in mind to give them the best of their budget.

Our team belongs to the Andaman Islands. They were born and brought up here in the islands. Some of them are from South Andaman, some from Middle Andaman and some from Little Andaman. The goal of our team is core challenging. We are very much dedicated to provide the best experiences of the Andaman Islands.
