About Us

Welcome to the world of Take Trip....

Based in Kolkata and Port Blair, Take A Trip is a leading Andaman Destination Management Company delivering bespoke experience for corporate clients. Since inception, we provide B2B catering services to our corporate clients keeping in mind to give them the best of their budget.

Our team belongs to the Andaman Islands. They were born and brought up here in the islands. Some of them are from South Andaman, some from Middle Andaman and some from Little Andaman. The goal of our team is core challenging. We are very much dedicated to provide the best experiences of the Andaman Islands. So far, the team has done eminently well in the islands and travellers do love the way we operate our tours. We are the company in India to bring transparent pricing in the Andaman Islands tour.

We are specialise in providing best of hotels, unique destinations, complete with premium transportation, an exclusive range of actives, exceptional food, plus art & culture experiences not normally accessible to the guest.

Apart from Andaman we provide services in Kolkata, Sikkim, Darjeeling, Assam etc. With a team of seasoned professionals which will offer quality service.

Whether it's a corporate tour, romantic delight, adventurous junkies or just a Chota Break, we have ample of tour choices to suit the most of your taste, requirement and budget. So Hurry up and book a Holiday with Take A Trip!
