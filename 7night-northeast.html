Northeast

7 NIGHT NORTH BENGAL & SIKKIM

SAMPLE ITINERARY: (KALIMPONG 1N - GANGTOK 2N - PELLING 2N - DARJEELING 2N)

NJP RLY STATION / IXB AIRPORT ARRIVAL - KALIMPONG (80 KMS. / 3 HRS.)

Day 01:  Assistance on arrival at New Jalpaiguri Railway Station / Bagdogra International Airport and transfer to Kalimpong (4,091 Ft.). On arrival, check-in to the hotel and rest of the day at leisure. Overnight at Kalimpong.

KALIMPONG SIGHTSEEING - GANGTOK (100 KMS. / 7 HRS.)

Day 02: After breakfast enjoy a half day Kalimpong city tour and visit Mangal Dham, Deolo Hill, Golf Course, Durpin Dara Hills. Later take transfer to Gangtok (5,500 Ft.). On arrival check-in to your hotel. Overnight at Gangtok.

EXCURSION TO TSOMGO LAKE AND BABA MANDIR (53 KMS. / 3 HRS. ONE-WAY)

Day 03:  Post breakfast, start for an excursion to Tsomgo Lake (12,400 ft. / 37 Kms. / 02 Hrs. from Gangtok) and to Baba Mandir (13,200 ft. / 16 Kms. / 01 Hrs. from Tsomgo Lake). Afternoon back to Gangtok. Overnight stay at Gangtok.

GANGTOK - PELLING VIA NAMCHI (150 KMS. / 7 HRS.)

Day 04:  After breakfast, start for Pelling (6,300 Ft. / 120 Kms. / 5 Hrs.) via Namchi to visit Siddheshwar Dham (Chardham) and Sai Temple. On arrival check-in to your hotel. Overnight stay at Pelling.

PELLING FULL DAY SIGHTSEEING WITH SKYWALK

Day 05:  After breakfast, start for a full day sightseeing tour. In first half, cover Darap village, Rimbi waterfalls, Khecheopalri Lake (Wishfulling Lake) and Khangchendzongha waterfalls. In the second half cover the Pemayangtse monastery, Rabdantse ruins (2nd capital of Sikkim), Sang-Ngag Choling (Skywalk), New helipad ground. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay at Pelling.

PELLING - DARJEELING (80 KMS. / 4 HRS.)

Day 06: After breakfast, transfer to Darjeeling (6,950 Ft.) via Singla Check post. On arrival, check-in to the hotel and rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

DARJEELING FULL DAY SIGHTSEEING WITH TIGER HILL

Day 07: Early morning (4:00 AM) visit Tiger Hill and then back to hotel. After breakfast, starting at 8.30 AM, visit Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, P.N. Zoological Park (Thursday close), Tenzing Rock, Ropeway, Tibetan Refugee self- help Centre (Sunday closed), Tea Garden (outer view), Ghoom Monastery and Batasia Loop. Evening free for shopping or leisure. Overnight stay at Darjeeling.

DARJEELING - NJP RLY STATION / IXB AIRPORT (75 KMS. / 3 HRS.)

Day 08: After breakfast Checkout from hotel and take, the departure transfers to New Jalpaiguri Railway Station/ Bagdogra International Airport for your onward journey.
